About me

A journey that
took its own route

My interest in computers started back in grade 10, but life had other plans. After losing my father, I stayed close to home and studied biology — a path chosen by circumstance, not by heart.

After completing my 12th grade, I made the decision to step away from premed and go after what I had always wanted. I prepared for IOE, then discovered BSc. CSIT — and knew it was the right fit.

I am now a first-semester CSIT student at Patan Multiple Campus, building the foundation I always wanted. It took longer than expected, but I am exactly where I am supposed to be.
